About

This is a space for people of all ages to openly and anonymously share how life crime has impacted their lives and the lives of those around them. Everyone is encouraged to share their experiences in the form of a letter. It could be a letter about how knife crime has impacted you, your friends, your family, or even just your thoughts and feelings.
This idea came into being as a result of the Charlie's Promise, a group which educates young people on the effect crime has on the victim's family and friends, and the wider communities. In this spirit we hope that people can learn about the impact of knife crime, through the words of those most effected by it.
If you or anyone you know is affected by knife crime, please contact charliespromise.org
